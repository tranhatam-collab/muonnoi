## Approval Workflow Types

### 1. Brand Change Approval

**Scope:** Brand changes (logo, colors, typography, voice, tone)

**Workflow:**
1. Submit Brand Change Request
2. Brand Guardian Review
3. Founder Approval (if major change)
4. Implement Brand Change
5. Brand Compliance Audit

**Timeline:** 1-3 weeks

**Approver:** Brand Guardian (AI Agent) + Founder (if major change)

---

## Approval Workflow Levels

### Level 1: Self-Approval

**Scope:** Minor brand changes, minor brand assets, minor brand content

**Approver:** Brand Guardian (AI Agent)

**Timeline:** 24-48 hours

**Criteria:**
- No major brand changes
- No major brand assets
- No major brand content
- Consistent with brand guidelines

---

### Level 2: Brand Guardian Approval

**Scope:** Major brand changes, major brand assets, major brand content

**Approver:** Brand Guardian (AI Agent)

**Timeline:** 48-72 hours

**Criteria:**
- Major brand changes
- Major brand assets
- Major brand content
- Consistent with brand guidelines

---

### Level 3: Founder Approval

**Scope:** Critical brand changes, critical brand assets, critical brand content, brand campaigns

**Approver:** Founder

**Timeline:** 72-96 hours

**Criteria:**
- Critical brand changes
- Critical brand assets
- Critical brand content
- Brand campaigns
- Founder approval required

---

## Approval Workflow Escalation

### Escalation Rules

**Level 1 → Level 2:**
- Brand Guardian escalates to Founder if:
  - Major brand change
  - Major brand asset
  - Major brand content
  - Brand campaign

**Level 2 → Level 3:**
- Brand Guardian escalates to Founder if:
  - Critical brand change
  - Critical brand asset
  - Critical brand content
  - Brand campaign

**Level 3:**
- Founder makes final decision
