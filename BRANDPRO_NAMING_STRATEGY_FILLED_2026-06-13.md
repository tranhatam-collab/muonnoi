## Brand Name

### VI (Vietnamese)

**Brand Name:** Muôn Nơi

**Meaning:** "Everywhere" / "All places"

**Rationale:**
- Short, memorable
- Easy to pronounce
- Meaningful in Vietnamese
- Reflects vision of connecting people everywhere

**Phonetic:** Muôn Nơi (muôn-nơi)

**Tone:** Friendly, inclusive, welcoming

---

### EN (English)

**Brand Name:** Muon Noi

**Meaning:** "Everywhere" / "All places" (transliteration)

**Rationale:**
- Consistent with Vietnamese
- Easy to pronounce for English speakers
- Maintains brand identity across languages

**Phonetic:** Muon Noi (muon-noi)

**Tone:** Friendly, inclusive, welcoming
