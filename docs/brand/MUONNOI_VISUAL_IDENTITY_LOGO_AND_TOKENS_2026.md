## MN mark rules
Use `MN` for:
- favicon
- app icon
- small badge
- mobile compact header
- avatar-like identity stamp

Do not use `MN` for:
- first public mention
- legal copy
- press headline
- first homepage H1

## Clear space and minimum size
Clear space:
- keep at least the width of the `M` around the mark.

Minimum digital size:
- `MN` mark: 32px wide.
- full wordmark: 96px wide.

Below minimum:
- use `MN` mark only.

## Typography
Current web implementation may continue existing stack until font asset lock.

Brand target for Vietnamese:
- Display: Be Vietnam Pro, 700/800.
- Body: Be Vietnam Pro, 400/500/600.
- Mono: JetBrains Mono or equivalent.

Rule:

- Headline line-height must leave room for accents.
- Do not ship a font that clips `ố`, `ứ`, `ỡ`, `Đ`, `đ`.

## Motion
Muonnoi motion signature:
- horizontal voice sweep
- slow, calm, non-distracting
- reduced motion respected

Do not use:
- vertical scan as the primary signature
- heavy logo animation
- decorative motion that hides content state

## Visual QA gate
Before a page is visual-brand-ready:
- MN mark renders.
- Wordmark spelling is correct.
- No conflicting logo or palette.
- Azure is primary signal.
- Gold appears only for proof/trust.
- Language label is full text.
- Text does not overlap on mobile.
- Vietnamese diacritics render correctly.
