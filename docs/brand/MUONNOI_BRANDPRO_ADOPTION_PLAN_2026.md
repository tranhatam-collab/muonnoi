## Muonnoi application decision
Muonnoi keeps one master brand across all surfaces.

Master brand:
- `Muôn Nơi`

Allowed meaning layer:
- `Muôn Nơi` = many places.
- `Muốn Nói` = want to speak.
- `Muon Noi` = ASCII form for domain/code/search.
- `MN` = compact mark and internal shorthand.

Rejected model:
- Do not create separate brands per subdomain.
- Do not make `lamviec`, `dulich`, `family`, `dautu`, `nhachung` independent brand systems.
- Do not create unrelated logos, palettes or tone per subdomain.

## Brand architecture
Global core:
- Muôn Nơi as master brand.
- Domain: `muonnoi.org`.
- Promise: hạ tầng số cho đời sống thật.
- Operating principles: social-first, privacy-first, trust-first, module-first, life-first.
- Claim rule: only say what the system can prove.
- Tone: clear, human, grounded, non-hype.
- Mark: `MN`.

Local/module expression:
- Subdomains may have a descriptor, icon, image style and accent emphasis.
- Subdomains must keep the Muôn Nơi wordmark, MN mark, shared language and route-back links.
- Subdomains may not change master claim, legal posture or trust rules.
