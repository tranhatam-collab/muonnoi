# MUONNOI BRAND STYLE LOCK · 2026

## Official names
Primary public name:
- `Muôn Nơi`

Meaning layer:
- `Muốn Nói` may be used only when explaining the brand idea.
- `Muon Noi` is the ASCII search/code/domain form.
- `MN` is the compact mark, app icon shorthand and internal shorthand.

Master domain:
- `muonnoi.org`

## Correct usage
Use `Muôn Nơi` in Vietnamese public copy.

Use `Muon Noi` only when:
- ASCII context requires it.
- Domain, code, handle, slug or search artifact needs no diacritics.
- English text needs a readable Latin fallback.

Use `MN` only when:
- Logo mark.
- App icon.
- Badge or very small UI.
- Internal team shorthand.

## Incorrect usage
Do not publish:
- `muonnoi` as visible brand name in body copy.
- `MUONNOI` as normal text.
- `MuônNơi` without space.
- `Muonnoi` as public-facing brand name.
- `Muốn Nơi`.
- `Muôn Nói`.
- `MN` as a replacement for the brand name in first mention.

## First mention rules
Vietnamese:
- First mention: `Muôn Nơi`.
- Optional explanation: `Muôn Nơi cũng gợi âm "Muốn Nói": nơi con người được nói điều thật và được lắng nghe.`

English:
- First mention: `Muon Noi`.
- Optional explanation: `Muon Noi is the ASCII form of Muôn Nơi, a Vietnamese name meaning many places and echoing the phrase want to speak.`

## Product and module naming
Use master brand plus module descriptor.

Correct:
- `Muôn Nơi Social Core`
- `Muôn Nơi Life Quest OS`
- `Muôn Nơi Trust Layer`
- `Muôn Nơi Mobile`
- `Muôn Nơi Docs`
- `Muôn Nơi Work`
- `Muôn Nơi Travel`
- `Muôn Nơi Family`

Avoid:
- `MN Social` as first mention.
- `LifeQuest` without space in public copy.
- `Dulich Muonnoi` as a public product name.
- Separate names that hide the master brand.

## Subdomain style
Subdomain pages must show:
- `Muôn Nơi` or `Muon Noi` first.
- Module descriptor second.
- Link back to `muonnoi.org`.
- Link to `docs.muonnoi.org` when docs exist.
- Link to `/security/` or trust policy for sensitive flows.

Example:
- `Muôn Nơi · Làm Việc`
- `Muôn Nơi · Du Lịch`
- `Muôn Nơi · Gia Đình`
- `Muôn Nơi · Đầu Tư`

## Language labels
Visible language controls must say:
- `Tiếng Việt`
- `English`

Never use visible:
- `VI`
- `EN`
- `VI/EN`

## Claim rules
Allowed:
- `đang xây dựng`
- `đã live`
- `đã kiểm tra route`
- `đang tuyển chọn`
- `mục tiêu`
- `kế hoạch`

Restricted unless evidence exists:
- numbers of hosts, pilots, users or partners
- legal partner names
- tourism board names
- receipt hash claims
- payment/email production claims

Forbidden:
- ROI promises.
- guaranteed earning.
- token-first economy.
- addictive-feed wording.
- fake urgency.
- world-class, revolutionary, guaranteed, instant, magic.
