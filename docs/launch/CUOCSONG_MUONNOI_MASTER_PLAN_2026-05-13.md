# CUOC SONG MUONNOI MASTER PLAN · 2026-05-13

## Verdict

CUOCSONG_LAYER_READY_FOR_TEAM_PLANNING

`Cuộc Sống Muôn Nơi` becomes the living-practice layer of the Muôn Nơi ecosystem. It is not a separate brand, not a replacement for Người Việt Muôn Nơi, and not a public-service provider for legal, financial, medical or immigration advice.

Its job is to help people prepare for a more grounded life across places: where to stay, how to work, what to learn, how to join a community, how to give and receive responsibly, and how to record real-life progress through Muôn Nơi receipts when the proof layer is ready.

## Core Definition

Cuộc Sống Muôn Nơi is:

- A practical living guide for people who want to live, learn, work and connect across places.
- A community readiness path before people join real pilots.
- A content and intake layer that routes users to the right Muôn Nơi module.
- A claim-safe rewrite of the older `cuocsongmuonnoi.com/.net` material.
- A future Life Quest surface when proof, receipt and host verification are ready.

Cuộc Sống Muôn Nơi is not:

- A visa, immigration, legal, investment, loan, medical, therapy or crisis-support provider.
- A promise of relocation, income, accommodation, free meals, success or financial return.
- A direct fundraising page.
- A replacement for the payment/email gate or legal review gate.

## Product Pillars

### 1. Định hướng cuộc sống

Help users understand the Muôn Nơi way of living:

- Sống thật ở địa điểm thật.
- Học bằng việc thật.
- Kết nối với cộng đồng thật.
- Tạo giá trị trước khi nhận quyền lợi.
- Tôn trọng luật, văn hóa và điều kiện địa phương.

### 2. Sống ở nhiều nơi

Provide non-regulated orientation:

- How to evaluate a place before staying longer.
- How to prepare budget, health, documents and family constraints without giving legal or financial advice.
- How to read local context and avoid romanticized relocation claims.
- How to choose between short stay, pilot stay, work stay and community stay.

### 3. Làm việc và cộng tác

Route users to `lamviec.muonnoi.org` and future work tools:

- Remote-work readiness checklist.
- Local contribution model.
- Skill exchange and collaboration etiquette.
- Work proof and receipt path after API/proof gate is ready.

### 4. Học tập và trải nghiệm

Route users to learning and Life Quest paths:

- Learning through place, task and community.
- Mentor/host-guided practice.
- Reflection and receipt after real action.
- No claim of guaranteed scholarship, job or certificate unless a verified partner provides it.

### 5. Cộng đồng sống

Connect to Người Việt, Nhà Chung and community pilots:

- Community house principles.
- Shared-space etiquette.
- Safety and responsibility rules.
- Host verification before public promotion.

### 6. Cho và nhận

Define a non-token, non-speculative value exchange:

- Give skill, time, local knowledge, care, documentation or hosting.
- Receive learning, connection, guidance, verified opportunity or community support.
- No earning promise.
- No reward claim before proof/reward gates pass.

### 7. Hỗ trợ toàn cầu

Create a safe directory layer:

- Verified links to official sources, partner pages and internal guides.
- No direct regulated advice unless a qualified, approved partner is listed.
- Every regulated topic must have a disclaimer and escalation route.

### 8. Nhà Chung / Đà Lạt pilot continuity

Keep Đà Lạt as a pilot narrative, not an unverified promise:

- Public copy may say Muôn Nơi is preparing/piloting local life experiences in Đà Lạt only when evidence exists.
- Fixed price, fundraising, free accommodation and guaranteed support claims are blocked.
- Public routes must link to claim-safe pilot pages, not old sales copy.

## Bilingual Rule

All public pages must support:

- `Tiếng Việt` as the written Vietnamese language label.
- `English` as the written English language label.

Do not use `VI`, `EN`, flags, shorthand-only buttons or mixed-language navigation labels. Each language version must be a complete page, not an auto-translation stub.

## Content Voice

Write in the Muôn Nơi voice:

- Human, clear, practical.
- No hype.
- No financial promise.
- No immigration, legal, medical or therapy advice.
- No "unlimited freedom" language that suggests avoiding law or responsibility.
- Every claim must map to evidence, route, policy or clear future-state wording.

Recommended public H1:

- Vietnamese: `Cuộc Sống Muôn Nơi`
- English: `Life Across Places`

Recommended subtitle:

- Vietnamese: `Một lớp hướng dẫn đời sống thật cho người muốn sống, học, làm việc và kết nối ở nhiều nơi một cách có trách nhiệm.`
- English: `A practical living layer for people who want to live, learn, work and connect across places responsibly.`

## Data And Intake

Phase 1 intake is content-only:

- No payment.
- No donation form.
- No investment form.
- No sensitive health, visa, legal or identity collection.
- Contact form can collect name, email, topic and message only after email delivery evidence is ready.

Phase 2 intake after email gate:

- Add interest routing by topic.
- Add consent checkbox.
- Store minimal inquiry record.
- Send receipt email only after email production evidence passes.

Phase 3 after proof/reward gate:

- Add Life Quest receipt for completed real-world tasks.
- Add host/mentor verification flow.
- Add limited private dashboard link to `app.muonnoi.org`.
