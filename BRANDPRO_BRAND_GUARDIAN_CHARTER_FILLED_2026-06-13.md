## Brand Guardian Authority

### Authority Levels

**Level 1: Brand Guidelines**
- Authority: Enforce brand guidelines
- Scope: All brand usage
- Approval: Self-approval

**Level 2: Brand Changes**
- Authority: Approve/reject brand changes
- Scope: Brand changes (logo, colors, typography, voice, tone)
- Approval: Founder approval for major changes

**Level 3: Brand Violations**
- Authority: Escalate brand violations
- Scope: Brand violations
- Approval: Founder approval for escalation

## Brand Guardian Workflow

### Brand Change Request Workflow

**Step 1: Submit Brand Change Request**
- Submitter: Any team member
- Content: Brand change request form
- Evidence: Before/after screenshots, rationale

**Step 2: Brand Guardian Review**
- Reviewer: Brand Guardian (AI Agent)
- Timeline: 24-48 hours
- Action: Approve/reject brand change

**Step 3: Founder Approval (if major change)**
- Approver: Founder
- Timeline: 48-72 hours
- Action: Approve/reject brand change

**Step 4: Implement Brand Change**
- Implementer: Design/Dev team
- Timeline: 1-2 weeks
- Action: Implement brand change

**Step 5: Brand Compliance Audit**
- Auditor: Brand Guardian (AI Agent)
- Timeline: 1 week after implementation
- Action: Audit brand compliance

---

### Brand Violation Workflow

**Step 1: Detect Brand Violation**
- Detector: Brand Guardian (AI Agent) or team member
- Content: Brand violation report
- Evidence: Screenshots, description

**Step 2: Brand Guardian Review**
- Reviewer: Brand Guardian (AI Agent)
- Timeline: 24-48 hours
- Action: Escalate to Founder

**Step 3: Founder Review**
- Reviewer: Founder
- Timeline: 48-72 hours
- Action: Approve escalation/reject escalation

**Step 4: Fix Brand Violation**
- Implementer: Design/Dev team
- Timeline: 1-2 weeks
- Action: Fix brand violation

**Step 5: Brand Compliance Audit**
- Auditor: Brand Guardian (AI Agent)
- Timeline: 1 week after fix
- Action: Audit brand compliance
